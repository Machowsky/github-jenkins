Main services we provide

Our services are the best in town, we provide great quality baked products.

Lots of Bread

We bake and deliver fresh bread seven days a week to our cafes, farmers’ markets and grocery stored throughout the NY city.

Cakes

We are dedicated to making cakes that are both moist and flavorful. All of our cakes are frosted and decorated with precision.

Cookies

Our sumptuous sugar cookies are a labor of love. They are made from scratch using only the finest ingredients and extracts.

Muffins

Our muffins can be decorated to suit any occasion. From birthdays to weddings, our muffins are the perfect single-serving confection.

About

Bakery was formed in 2014 after the merger of the two largest bakeries in the country; The Creative Muffin and Westcoast Bread. With over 200 employees, there isn’t a job too big or too ambitious.
